The Best Movies According to Matteo

My top 3 movies of all time.

Pulp Fiction

"Pulp Fiction" is a cult classic film directed by Quentin Tarantino, released in 1994. Set in a non-linear narrative, the movie weaves multiple interconnected stories featuring various intriguing characters. It explores themes of crime, violence, redemption, and dark humor.

Kill Bill Vol. 1 & Vol. 2

"Kill Bill: Volume 1" and "Kill Bill: Volume 2" are two parts of a thrilling and action-packed movie series directed by Quentin Tarantino.

Interstellar

Interstellar is a sci-fi film directed by Christopher Nolan. The story revolves around a group of astronauts who embark on a daring interstellar journey through a newly discovered wormhole near Saturn. Their mission is to find a new habitable planet for humanity, as Earth is facing an impending environmental catastrophe.
